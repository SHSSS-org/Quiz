Question 1 of 5
Which of the following is the standard unit of measurement for energy in the International System of Units (SI)?
Newton
Joule
Watt
Pascal
Correct Answer:
Joule. The joule (J) is the derived unit of energy in the International System of Units. It is equal to the energy transferred to an object when a force of one newton acts on that object in the direction of the force through a distance of one meter.
